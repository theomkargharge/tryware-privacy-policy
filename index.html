TryWare AI ("we," "our," or "us") is developed and operated by CrazzyAppz. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your information when you use the TryWare AI mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1 Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you sign in with Google Sign-In, we may collect your name, email address, and profile photo URL to create and manage your account.

b) Photos You Upload

To generate AI virtual try-on results, you upload a photo of yourself and a clothing image. These images are securely transmitted to our AI processing servers solely to generate your requested results and are not stored permanently.

c) Generated Images

Generated images are saved locally on your device. Temporary generated files inside the App may be automatically removed after seven (7) days unless you explicitly save them to your device gallery.

d) Payment Information

Payments are securely processed through Razorpay. We do not collect or store your debit card, credit card, UPI PIN, banking credentials, or other payment information. We store your purchased credit balance and transaction history.

e) Device & Usage Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs

f) Deletion Fingerprint (Anti-Abuse)

Upon account deletion, we store a one-way cryptographic fingerprint (HMAC-SHA256 hash) of your email address. This fingerprint is not reversible — no email address can be recovered from it. It is used solely to prevent free-trial abuse while complying with all applicable privacy laws.

🔒 Your raw email address is never retained after deletion. Only its irreversible hash is stored. This is GDPR-safe by design.

8 Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your TryWare AI account at any time via two methods:

Method 1 — In-App (Instant)

  1. Open the TryWare AI app.
  2. Tap Settings (bottom navigation bar).
  3. Scroll to the ACCOUNT section.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm in the warning dialog. Your account is deleted immediately.

Method 2 — Web / Email Request

Email us at crazzyapp28@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request" and the email linked to your account. We will process it within 7 business days.
